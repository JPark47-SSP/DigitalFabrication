Smart Water Dispenser

A personalized water dispenser designed for your everyday health. Every component in life starts with water.

Demo Video

Motivation - Why did I build this?

As an athlete and student, I often found myself forgetting to hydrate properly. I created this Smart Water Dispenser to promote healthy hydration habits with automation, simplicity, and reliability.

Features

  • Scheduled automatic dispensing
  • Volume control (fixed output)
  • LCD status display
  • Buzzer hydration reminders
  • Manual button mode
  • Sensor-based failsafe cutoff

Development Process

Step 1: Planning & Goal Setting

Defined objectives: automate hydration, fixed volume dispensing, and safety via sensors. I sketched goals and required parts before writing a single line of code.

Step 2: Electronics Prototyping

Tested each component (relay, ultrasonic sensor, pump) on a breadboard. Ensured compatibility with Seeeduino XIAO ESP32 and ran test sketches using Arduino IDE.

Step 3: Arduino Code

Used millis() for non-blocking time logic, handled button press logic, and wrote safety check functions using the ultrasonic sensor. Buzzer and LCD were integrated for UX feedback.

Step 4: CAD & 3D Printing

Modeled parts using Fusion 360. Printed with PLA and tested fit with electronics. Nozzle mount and bottle holder were refined for stability and waterproofing.

Step 5: Assembly & Testing

All components were mounted inside the case and wiring was soldered to perfboard. Final testing included volume calibration (~200ml per 3s), sensor delay tuning, and pump vibration checks.

Step 6: Materials Used

  • Seeeduino XIAO ESP32
  • Relay module
  • HC-SR04 ultrasonic sensor
  • LCD 16x2 I2C
  • Pushbutton + piezo buzzer
  • Submersible pump
  • 3D printed nozzle mount + holder
